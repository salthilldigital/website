Accessible digital skills training using VR, AR, and AI technologies to bridge the digital divide in Slough and surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Salt Hill Digital provides accessible and engaging digital skills training using emerging technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Artificial Intelligence (AI). We deliver hands-on workshops, community events, and immersive training programs aimed at increasing digital literacy, confidence, and career readiness among underrepresented communities in Slough and the surrounding areas.
